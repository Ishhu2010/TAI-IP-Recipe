Special Shahi

Shahi Paneer is a popular North Indian dish made with paneer (Indian cottage cheese) cooked in a rich, creamy gravy. The gravy is typically made with a blend of tomatoes, onions, cashews, and various aromatic spices like cardamom, cinnamon, and cloves. It's known for its luxurious texture and mildly sweet, flavorful taste, making it a favorite among vegetarians and non-vegetarians alike. Shahi Paneer is often served with naan, rice, or roti, making it a delightful and satisfying meal.

prep time

30 min.

Cook time

15 min.

servings

6 servings.

Tags : beef breakfast Panner food

instructions

step 1

Prepare the gravy: Blend tomatoes, onions, cashews, and spices into a smooth paste.

step 2

Cook the paste: Saut√© the paste in oil or ghee until it releases oil and turns aromatic.

step 3

Add paneer: Add paneer cubes to the cooked paste, along with cream and water, then simmer.

step 4

Garnish and serve: Garnish with fresh cream and cilantro, and serve with naan or rice.

ingredients

Paneer - 250g, cubed, Tomatoes - 2, chopped

Onion - 1, chopped, Cashews - 10-12, soaked

Ginger-Garlic Paste - 1 tbsp, Green Cardamom - 2-3 pods

Cinnamon Stick - 1 small piece, Turmeric Powder - 1/2 tsp
